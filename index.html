Champion speeds

First-tier boots provide +25 movement speed. With a pair of boots, Anivia can catch up to Akali (until Akali buys a pair).

Most second-tier boots provide +45 movement speed, plus some other benefit.

Boots of Swiftness provide +55 movement speed and make slows 25% less effective.

Boots of Mobility give you +25 movement speed in combat and +115 out of combat.
